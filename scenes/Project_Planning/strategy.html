Welcome to your digital story ICTGlobe!

Since we care about you, our valued employees, and our organizational needs and requirements, we took some time remind you about YOU.


Here is what you should allready know...

Situated in Centurion and Stellenbosch, South Africa, ICTGlobe stands as a premier Tier 1 ICASA-licensed telecommunications and end-to-end service provider.


Operating on a foundation of agility and innovation, we don't believe in one-size-fits-all technology, choosing instead to accelerate business growth through our highly customized ICT ecosystems.


Our dedicated team at ICTGlobe owns and operates our very own state-of-the-art Tier 3 data centre and interconnected voice platform. We specialize in deploying massive, mission-critical infrastructure—from crystal-clear corporate VoIP and 3CX unified communications to resilient business fibre, cloud hosting environments, and advanced cybersecurity frameworks.

Directly peered with over 95% of South African networks, we ensure the lowest possible latency and back our services with a strict 99.999% network uptime guarantee.


With over two decades of experience in the fast-evolving telecommunications landscape, ICTGlobe strive to eliminate operational friction and turn technology into an effortless driver of growth.


Committed to absolute business continuity, our infrastructure features twin backup generators and a 24/7 technical team. ICTGlobe provides our clients with one unified strategy and a single point of ultimate accountability, so that they can connect, collaborate, and lead with absolute confidence.
